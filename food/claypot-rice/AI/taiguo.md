泰國椰香雞肉煲仔飯
Thai Coconut Chicken Claypot Rice

食材準備：

米、雞腿肉、香茅、檸檬葉、椰漿、魚露、生抽、青檸、小米辣、洋蔥、大蒜、生薑、鹽、食用油、香菜。

製作步驟：
米浸泡後放入砂煲，加適量水和火山石煮。香茅、檸檬葉切碎，小米辣切圈，青檸榨汁，洋蔥、大蒜、生薑切末。
雞腿肉去骨切塊，用魚露、生抽、部分香茅碎、檸檬葉碎、蒜末、薑末醃製。
鍋中倒油，炒香洋蔥末，加入醃製好的雞肉翻炒至變色，倒入椰漿和適量水，放入剩餘香茅碎、檸檬葉碎，煮至湯汁濃稠。
米飯煮至表面無水時，倒入雞肉和湯汁，小火懟熟。出鍋前擠上青檸汁，撒上小米辣圈和香菜。

Ingredients:
Rice, chicken thighs, lemongrass, lemon leaves, coconut milk, fish sauce, light soy sauce, lime, chili pepper, onion, garlic, ginger, salt, cooking oil, coriander.

Steps:
After soaking the rice, put it into a clay pot, add appropriate amount of water and volcanic stones and cook. Chop lemongrass and lemon leaves, cut chili into rings, squeeze lime juice, and mince onion, garlic and ginger.
Debone the chicken thighs and cut them into pieces. Marinate them with fish sauce, light soy sauce, some chopped lemongrass, chopped lemon leaves, minced garlic, and minced ginger.
Pour oil into the pan, sauté chopped onion, add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it changes color, pour in coconut milk and appropriate amount of water, add the remaining chopped lemongrass and lemon leaves, and cook until the soup is thick.
When the rice is cooked until there is no water on the surface, pour in the chicken and soup and simmer over low heat. Squeeze lime juice on top and sprinkle with chili rings and coriander before serving.
