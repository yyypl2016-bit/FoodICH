星空漸層紫薯煲仔飯
Starry Sky Gradient Purple Potato Claypot Rice

食材準備：
米、紫薯、蝶豆花、牛奶、冰糖、椰蓉、葡萄乾

製作步驟：
米洗淨浸泡，紫薯蒸熟後壓成泥狀。蝶豆花用熱水泡出顏色，加入牛奶和冰糖煮至冰糖融化，製成藍色牛奶液。
將部分米放入砂煲，加入紫薯泥和適量水，用火山石煮。待米飯煮至半熟時，在砂煲一側倒入藍色牛奶液，繼續煮至飯熟。
出鍋後撒上椰蓉和葡萄乾，形成星空漸層的效果

Ingredients:
Rice, purple sweet potato, butterfly pea flower, milk, rock sugar, coconut flakes, and raisins

Steps:
Wash and soak the rice, steam the purple sweet potato and mash it into a paste. Soak the butterfly pea flowers in hot water to bring out their color, add milk and rock sugar and cook until the rock sugar melts to make blue milk liquid.
Put some rice into a clay pot, add purple sweet potato puree and appropriate amount of water, and cook with volcanic stones. When the rice is half cooked, pour blue milk liquid on one side of the clay pot and continue cooking until the rice is cooked.
After serving, sprinkle with shredded coconut and raisins to create a starry sky gradient effect
