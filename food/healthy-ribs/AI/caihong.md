彩虹蔬果臘口味煲仔飯
 
Rainbow Vegetable and Fruit Sauce Clay pot Rice

食材準備：
米、臘腸、胡蘿蔔、玉米、青豆、香菇、紫薯、南瓜、綠花椰菜、生抽、蠔油、鹽、食用油、黑胡椒粉。

製作步驟：
米洗淨浸泡半小時，紫薯、南瓜去皮切丁。將紫薯丁、南瓜丁與米混合，放入砂煲，加入適量水，用火山石煮。
臘腸切片，胡蘿蔔去皮切丁，香菇泡發後切丁。鍋中倒油，放入臘腸煸炒出油，加入胡蘿蔔丁、香菇丁、玉米、青豆翻炒，加少許鹽和黑胡椒粉調味。
米飯煮至七八成熟時，鋪上炒好的臘味蔬菜。西蘭花切成小朵，汆燙後擺放在砂煲邊緣。
繼續用小火燜煮至飯熟，關火後​​淋上生抽和蠔油混合的醬汁


Ingredients: 
Rice, sausage, carrots, corn, green beans, mushrooms, purple sweet potatoes, pumpkin, broccoli, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, cooking oil, black pepper

Steps:
Wash rice, soak for 30 mins. Dice purple sweet potato and pumpkin. Mix with rice in a clay pot, add water, and cook with volcanic stones.
Slice sausage, dice carrots and mushrooms. Stir-fry sausage in oil until oil releases, then add carrots, mushrooms, corn, and green beans. Season with salt and black pepper.
When rice is 70% cooked, add stir-fried vegetables. Blanch broccoli florets and place around the pot.
Simmer until rice is done. Turn off heat, pour soy and oyster sauce mixture on top.
