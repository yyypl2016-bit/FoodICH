客家釀豆腐是傳統的客家非遺菜式，承載著深厚的文化與情感。要在此基礎上創新，可以從食材搭配、製作工藝、呈現方式等多個角度出發。以下是一個創意菜的設計，融合現代健康飲食和創新概念：
創意菜名：花園釀豆腐盞
創意亮點：
健康升級：結合低脂、高纖的健康飲食概念，減少油脂，增加蔬果元素。
創新造型：將傳統釀豆腐製作成「花園」主題，豐富視覺體驗。
多元口感：融合豆腐的嫩滑、蔬果的清脆，以及釀料的鮮美。


Hakka stuffed tofu is a traditional Hakka intangible cultural heritage dish that carries a deep culture and emotion. To innovate on this basis, you can start from multiple angles such as ingredient matching, production process, and presentation method. The following is a design of a creative dish that combines 
modern healthy diet and innovative concepts:
Creative dish name: Garden stuffed tofu
Creative highlights:
Health upgrade: Combine the healthy diet concept of low fat and high fiber, reduce oil, and increase vegetable and fruit elements.
Innovative shape: Make traditional stuffed tofu into a "garden" theme to enrich the visual experience.
Multiple tastes: Combining the tenderness of tofu, the crispness of fruits and vegetables, and the deliciousness of stuffing.
