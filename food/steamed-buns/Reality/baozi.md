
包子是一道历史悠久的中国传统美食，深受人们喜爱。最早可追溯至古代，因其方便携带且营养丰富，曾是劳工和旅人常食之物。随着时间的推移，包子逐渐成为各地饮食文化中的重要组成部分，每个地区都发展出了独特的馅料和风味。

Baozi is a beloved dish with deep cultural roots in China. Originating from ancient times, these soft, fluffy buns were traditionally enjoyed as a convenient and nourishing meal by workers and travelers. Over the centuries, baozi became a staple across various regions, each developing unique variations in fillings and flavors.


在英国，“包子”常被误认为是台湾的荷叶包，一种夹着卤肉的折叠式蒸包。为了打破这一刻板印象，并向大众推广真正的中式包子，厨师包创立了包公包子品牌。他的目标不仅是改变英国人对包子的认知，更希望将这种美味又松软的传统美食传播得更远。如今，随着他的努力，越来越多的人开始了解并爱上真正的中式包子及其丰富的文化底蕴。

But in the UK, "baozi" is often mistaken for Taiwanese gua bao, the folded steamed buns commonly filled with pork belly. To challenge this misconception and introduce the authentic flavors of traditional Chinese baozi, chef Bao founded the brand Bao Gong Baozi. His mission is to reshape the British perception of baozi while sharing the delicious taste of these fluffy, steamed buns with a wider audience. Through his efforts, more people are discovering the rich heritage and irresistible flavors of real baozi.
