Steamed Soup Buns

Delicate translucent dumplings filled with fragrant broth and tender pork, a beloved dim sum delicacy

Historical Background

Dim Sum Heritage

These exquisite soup buns evolved during the golden era of Cantonese dim sum, representing centuries of culinary refinement. The technique of capturing savory broth within delicate wrappers showcases the mastery and precision that defines Cantonese cooking traditions.

Core Ingredients

What's Inside

  • Thin, translucent wheat dough wrapper
  • Ground pork or shrimp filling
  • Rich, gelatinous broth
  • Fresh ginger and scallions
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil
  • Aspic for broth gel

Artisan Technique

Preparation Method

Creating soup buns requires exceptional skill. Meat is combined with aspic jelly, wrapped in delicate dough and pleated by hand. Steaming melts the aspic into liquid broth that fills the bun. Each bun is a feat of culinary engineering, demanding precision timing and expert craftsmanship.
